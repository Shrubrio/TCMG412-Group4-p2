I’ve encountered love and confronted death. These are the two extreme ends of the spectrum of emotions that humans face. Not only have I endured these two extremes, I’ve also come into contact with much of the gray area in between. My name is Ja'Keveaun Langston Wallace, and I am the sum of all my experiences. When I was younger, I grew up in a broken family where laughter was key to my survival. So over time I developed the special ability to make others laugh. Whether it’s through laughter or a simple compliment, making a person smile has always been something I’ve cherished. I’m very compassionate and empathetic which can sometimes make things more difficult for me than they should be. Breaking someone’s heart by delivering bad news hurts me to my core, but I’m equipped to be able to do it from the literal decade of experience I have in trying to make people smile.
